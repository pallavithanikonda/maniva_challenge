Services

Our team of experienced professionals and our services meet the needs for digital product development of all types and sizes - from small startups to large firms - and deliver lasting changes with measurable growth. Get in touch with us today to learn how Maniva Digital can help you achieve your objectives.

APP & WEB DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT

Our team specializes in Mobile & Web design and development. We follow the best guidelines in UI/UX to deliver a responsive design for optimum performance. We can develop for iOS/Android with a full adaptation of graphics, text & pictures.

DIGITAL STRATEGY CONSULTATION

We examine what your organization is doing to stay relevant and competitive in this fast-paced digital world. We then strategize using smart digital tools and global resources in order to provide you with a dynamic implementation plan.

PERFORMANCE & DATA ANALYSIS

In collaboration with our global partners, we implement analytic tools into your App/Web such as Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Firebase, etc to continuously measure key parameters (visits, on-boarding, bounce rate, etc) of your business performance.

TECHNICAL SUPPORT & MAINTENANCE

Often, our clients have little time to manage their web/app. Our skilled staff can take care of this, providing their expertise 24/7. We make sure your platform is up to date with the latest upgrades for optimal performance over time.
